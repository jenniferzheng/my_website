About Me

Hi there, welcome to my personal website! I am a 19 year old computer programmer and I have been coding since my senior year of high school. Through my computer science course in high school, I discovered the logical and problem-solving process of coding. As the concepts clicked in my brain, I decided that I wanted to pursue this field in my college education and explore the possibilities that computer science offers. Fast forward a couple of years, and I am now a sophomore studying Computer Science and Business Administration at University of Southern California.

Through my studies here at SC, I have been able to develop my coding skills in C++ and build accumulative projects in my classes. While I have been able to build a solid foundation in these technical skills, I am excited to delve into learning different languages, realms of CS, and development skills. I plan to build these additional skills through personal projects, such as learning HTML/CSS through building this website. Furthermore, I plan to continue attending hack-a-thons (s/o to HACKSC as my first hack-a-thon) and taking additional technical electives through USC's IT Program using my free units. If you want to learn about my education, experience, and projects in more detail, check out the resume section of my website (link?) and GitHub.

Outside of my studies, I have various interests and hobbies that fill up my free time. For example, I love to travel and throughout my childhood, I have had the blessing of travelling around the country with my family. For as long as I remember, our family would plan at least one road trip every year to visit national parks and explore cities. Now, I have the itch to take a month-long trip to Europe to explore France, Italy, Greece, Spain, and the list goes on. Furthermore, I love being active, whether its through swimming competitively or hiking through the mountains. Especially through growing up in the Pacific Northwest, I have a great appreciation for the outdoors, and I am always up for a hike to discover the countless views, waterfalls, and beauty that Washington and Oregon holds.
